02

One story: magma ocean → water world.

PROTEUS simulates how rocky planets cool from fully molten beginnings into potentially habitable, water-bearing worlds. The identity encodes that single transition everywhere:

Magma red is the hot state — rare, high-energy, used for signals and hot data.
Void black is deep time and space — the default surface.
Ocean blue is the cool state — interactive, living, the destination.

Every artifact — a slide, a figure, the docs, a sticker — reads hot → cold, inside → out, past → future.

MAGMA · 4.5 GYR AGOVOIDOCEAN · TODAY
The glyph is the story in miniature: a molten core cooling outward through concentric rings into ice-blue. Hot inside, cool outside — never reversed.

05

Rules

DO
  • Reproduce the glyph only from master assets (assets/logo/).
  • Match the colorway to the surface: phase on Void/Paper, mono elsewhere.
  • Use glyph-only below 90px, and for avatars, favicons, stickers.
  • Keep 1× cap-height clearspace on all sides.
DON'T
  • Don't set the wordmark in any face but Sora, or in mixed case.
  • Don't reverse the gradient — cool core / hot rim is wrong physics.
  • Don't add glows, shadows, or outlines to the mark.
  • Don't place the phase glyph on mid-tone or busy surfaces.
  • Don't rebuild the glyph from circles, arcs, or AI generations.

MOTIFS
10

Rings, ramp, hairlines

Concentric rings
The glyph, oversized and cropped at edges, is the only decorative element. Use one per surface, at low prominence, never rotated.
Phase band
A thin (4–10px) horizontal ramp marks openings and closings: slide footers, page ends, section breaks. Always hot-left, cold-right.
Hairlines
Structure comes from 1px rules, not shadows or containers. A short Magma tick marks the active or highlighted item.
